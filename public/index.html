Now you've got friends in high-tech places

At Just Better Computers, we want to change what you expect from a computer service provider. We aim to live up to our name and be Just Better, in every way. For us, the customer really does come first. Here are some of our core principles:

Reliability

We earn your trust by doing what we say we'll do, putting your needs and best interests ahead of profits, and delivering exceptional service time after time. If you ever have a concern, we'll do everything we can to ensure your satisfaction. We love happy customers.

Promptness

We know you're in a hurry to get things back up and running, and we want to help. That's why we're open until 9 P.M. 7 days a week, keep many parts in stock, and offer cheap 1 day shipping on most other parts. This allows us to offer you an average turnaround time of 0-2 days.

Convenience

Getting your computer or other device fixed should be easy. We make sure it is by offering remote assistance when possible as well as free house calls and pickup/delivery service in Milledgeville. We will travel as far as 80 miles from Milledgeville for $10-$25 depending on distance.

Affordability

We believe in giving you a fair price, even if it means less profit for us. That's why we never charge more than $95 for labor. We also don't mark up our parts like other companies, meaning you always get the best price on parts, and we'll never try to sell you something you don't need.

Friendly Service

We don't believe professionalism has to be overly formal. We'd rather be treated like friends than customers, and we hope you would too. Lots of our customers have been with us for many years now and seem like family(See our Testimonials page). We look forward to meeting and helping you.

Charity

Just Better Computers wouldn't exist without you and many other great people. We feel very grateful that we have been fortunate enough to receive so much support, and we want to pay it forward. Every month, 10% of our profits will go to charitable donations and acts(See our Charity page).
